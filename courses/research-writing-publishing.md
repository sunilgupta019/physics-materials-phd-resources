> Communicate and publish your work effectively.

| MIT communication courses | MIT OCW | https://ocw.mit.edu |

## Workflow
1. Draft in LaTeX (Overleaf) using a journal template.
2. Preprint to arXiv (cond-mat) before/at submission.
3. Target reputable open-access venues (check DOAJ).
